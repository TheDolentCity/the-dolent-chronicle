The NSR Cauldron community zine **Sword&** has officially released issue 2! You can find all existing issues [on itch.io](https://itch.io/profile/nsrc). I was honored to be the Editor-In-Chief for this issue and thrilled to see how many submissions and volunteers we had. To all who contributed writing, editing, art, layout, and project management effort: thank you! This zine wouldn't exist without you!

## What's inside?

In this issue, you'll find:

- **Moon-lighting**: Lore and starter kits to help build a space wizard.
- **Alternatives to Spellbooks**: technology has advanced the Spell Book into the final frontier.
- **Space Wizard Familiars**: six wacky space wizard familiars.
- **Engram Scanner**: a short story where a wizard copies a mind to a new body with a ritual and titular scanner..
- **Most Wanted**: the galaxy's 6 most wanted space wizards.
- **Space Station Nous Eternal**: a gelatinous space dungeon filled to the brim with hazardous organic material.
- **Stellar Lichdom**: the blackest path of space wizardry. To destroy a being of this scale will be no common labor.
- **Tomb of the Star Mother**: a tomb cursed by an ancient diety? Or a crashed spaceship? Play to find out.

## What is the NSR Cauldron?

The NSR Cauldron is a community discord server for discussing, making, and playing tabletop roleplaying games. The community is many things to many people, but some commonly agreed on principles are:

1. The best game is the game you like to play, the way you like to play it.
1. Don't make myths about history. Look backward, think forward.
1. Hack a game together. Steal from everything. Never be satisfied with someone else's rules.
